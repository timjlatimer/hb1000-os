The geometry — a diamond split by the equator, blank

The vertical spine, pointed at one life:

  1. Your Prime — your own Ding in the Universe.
  2. Twin North Star Gate — every personal project answers "What's the Ding?" Prime check + Local check, or HALT → Park.
  3. You — the HB1000 of your own life, at the equator. Agents 80%, you 20%.
  4. Your Locals — Partner · Parent · Health · Work · Community · Money.
  5. Your personal Dingo card — 25 squares, PTK (Promises to Keep) at the centre.
  6. Your Nursery — your Cloud Butterflies, landed on Flypaper, waiting to graduate.
"The Dingo Card IS the operating system. Everything else is a field on it, a feed into it, or a score from it."
1 · Your Prime North Star — ONE per life

Your "Ding in the Universe" — your own Do-Before-I-Die. The litmus: if a project can't articulate its Ding, it's parked. Worked example: Tim's Prime.

2 · Your Local North Stars — many, each must rhyme with your Prime

Six lanes of one life. A Local is one sentence: who you serve in that lane and what "winning" looks like. Not identical to the Prime — rhyming with it.

3 · Your personal Dingo card — blank, PTK at the centre
80/20 roll-up (computed, never asserted)

Mean of your share over the 24 non-PTK squares. Over 20% turns the bar red — the covenant: agents 80%, you 20%.

Card "complete" checklist (computed)

Dingo Caller — "one neck to choke" populates the squares & approves · 20% | AI agent fleet — does the work · 80%. Centre square = PTK — Promises to Keep, the AI promise engine — track your promises in the PTK dual ledger.

4 · Your personal Nursery — Cloud Butterflies, inner ring

Ideas land here first (Flypaper). Score them later; only "Now" graduates to a card. Buckets: Nursery → Now / Next / Later / Park / Kill (a Kill gets a eulogy + a lesson — ideas merge, they never die).
