Inside a Dingo Card: a 5×5 grid of 25 activity squares; the centre square is PTK — Promises to Keep (the AI promise engine — never a blank giveaway). Every square carries KPIs, a red/yellow/green status, and an 80/20 human-vs-agent bar. Across the top a ticker-tape shows whether the building is winning. The card is the operating system — everything else is a field on it, a feed into it, or a score from it.
