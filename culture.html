Hard rules — doctrine, not décor
  • "Immune System, not a Police System." Catcher Car, not the Police Car. Every catch is a learning mechanism.
  • Every catch names the specific best practice breached and refracts through the Babel Bridge at the right Feynman altitude (Street / Boardroom / Architect).
  • The Dignity Test is a blocking gate: "Would you be comfortable if your mother went through this?"
  • No streak-shaming. No raw social-comparison mechanics (BP-060/061 — gamification and raw tracking are top motivation killers).
  • The internal game never touches Ruby-Red-facing surfaces (BP-063/066 — defaults hit low-income users 3× harder). Two surfaces, kept separate.
  • Joint human + agent score. AI co-racers can be caught too — and humans help repair them. Radical Collaboration.
  • Winner patterns propagate as Sourdough Starters: opt-in for the winner, anonymous by default for the recipient. "It removes ego from the equation."
  • Rate-limited interventions. Healthy immune system resolves 90% pre-fever; minimum effective dose per person (Immune Memory).
  • Master opt-in with an easy off switch. Nothing auto-applies. Nothing auto-sends. The human approves every move.
  • Sat–Thu work week; Friday is for celebration and reset.
